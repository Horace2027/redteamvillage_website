Call For DJs

The Red Team Offense Village is looking for DJs to play during the DEF CON 27 conference. Timeslots are limited so apply now. Be heard by hundreds of DEF CON attendances!

Equipment: The Red Team Offense Village will provide two CDJ CD players, two technic 1200 turntables, a multi-channel mixer and DJ quality monitor.

Style: All styles are welcome. This includes funk, hip-hop, house, techno, jungle, dubstep, country, 2 hours of bleeping sounds …. Whatever you want as long as it doesn’t anger listeners. The Red Team Offense Village has the right to end a DJ set early if the music is deemed offensive or disturbing to the environment. Outside of that, any style is welcome.
