THANK YOU Volunteers!!!

Do you want to help run the DEF CON Red Team Village? We have plenty of ways for you to help the cause!

NOTE: The DEF CON Red Team Village has ZERO Tolerance Policy for harassment of any kind. Volunteer, attendee, speaker, it doesn’t matter - any CoC violators will be ejected. Please review the DEF CON CoC at: https://www.defcon.org/html/links/dc-code-of-conduct.html

DEF CON Red Team Village slots include (but not limited to) Discord moderation, speaker introductions and logistics, helping staff with network administrative tasks, helping attendees, and other administrative tasks.

The DEF CON Red Team Offense Village volunteers are the heart of this village, they bring a wealth of expertise, experiences and uniqueness to help make us the DEF CON village of the year.
